How it works

The agent listens to TxODDS' live de-margined World Cup prices. When it spots a real market move it opens a small paper position — and then proves, on the Solana blockchain, that the exact price tick it acted on was genuinely published by the oracle: each tick is checked against a Merkle root committed on-chain every five minutes. Green "proved on-chain" badges link straight to the Solana explorer, so you don't have to trust the agent — you can verify it.
